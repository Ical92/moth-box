Notes

  • Flesh Damage heals by 0.5/s for as long as Flesh Healing is greater than zero. Flesh Healing decreases by 0.5/s but the rate can be decreased by clean gauze.
  • Both flesh damage and infection must be cleared for the wound to be fully treated. Flesh damage will never increase but the damage multiplier will still be present and infection will increase until infection is also treated.
  • Damage Multiplier multiplies incoming brute and burn damage to the limb that the wound is present on.
