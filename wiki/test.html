    Makosso-Warra is a corporate conglomerate conducting business and holding claims across the galaxy. The company was founded as a joint Bezuts-Sol venture in 280 FS, 30 years after first contact with the Solar Confederation. Makosso, based in the Sol African Canton, specialized in orbital communications & navigation technology; while Warra, based on Kalixcis, manufactured consumer electronics.
