    The Confederated League of Independent Planets (shortened to CLIP, and formerly known by a variety of names) is a group of colonies originating from the United Republics of Free Zohil, that banded together to form a sovereign nation. Initially formed from deserters of Free Zohil, it has since been remolded into a semi-united coalition of planets.

    Being a collection of multiple colonies who have faced many different experiences, the League has a extremely decentralized nature. One person from one planet will be completely different from another person from another planet. Enlisting is often a common way of meeting others in the League.

    2. History #

    • -98 FS

      The PGF (Pan-Gezenan Federation) places the first Kalixcian into space. The President of the Federation self-declares the PGF as the winner of the Second Kalixcian Space Race.

    • -73 FS

      The URFZ (United Republics of Free Zohil) puts the first Kalixcian on a Kalixcian moon, Toitek. Following this breakthrough, the Prime Minister of the Republic, declared that Zohil was the true winner of the space race.

    • -20 FS

      The URFZ establishes remote first contact with an alien species known as the Kepori. Despite initial skepticism, their claim is verified by a public release of undeniable proof.
